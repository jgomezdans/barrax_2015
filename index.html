    Material for NERC's Field Spectroscopy Course

    This site contains support material for the NERC course of Field Spectroscopy that took place in Albacete (Spain) in July 2015. This site contains my contributions to the course, mainly in the areas of radiative transfer (RT) modelling in the optical domain. In here, you can find some presentations, IPython notebooks with computational examples, etc.
